LikedIt Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 2026

LikedIt is a browser extension that highlights Instagram posts the user has already liked while browsing Instagram.

The extension processes information locally within the user's browser to determine whether a post has been liked and applies a visual highlight to those posts.

LikedIt does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data, browsing history, or account information. All functionality occurs locally on the user's device and no data is sent to external servers.

If the extension’s functionality changes in the future, this privacy policy will be updated accordingly.
